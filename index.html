4. Power Density: Decrypting the 3C Metric
Cell #1 (The Baseline): Achieved a massive ~1150-1200 mAh/g specific capacity at 0.01C, successfully utilizing the ultra-lean E/S = 3. Even with the kinetic drop-off to ~750 mAh/g at 0.1C, this capacity absolutely crushes legacy Li-ion.
Cell #4 (The Kinetic Armor): Tested under continuous 3C charge AND discharge (draining and refilling the entire battery in just 20 minutes continuously).
THE PHYSICS OF 3C:
To standard Li-ion engineers, "3C" sounds normal. But theoretical capacity dictates the absolute current. Legacy Li-ion (LFP) has a theoretical capacity of ~170 mAh/g, meaning 3C is ~0.51 A/g.

Lithium-Sulfur’s theoretical capacity is a massive 1675 mAh/g. Therefore, a 3C rate in Li-S pushes a staggering ~5.0 A/g—nearly 10 TIMES the electrical current density pushed through a standard EV battery.
Cell #4 sustained ~450 mAh/g under this sheer kinetic brutality for over 100 cycles. This unlocks extreme Power Densities (thousands of W/kg), paving the way for heavy-duty commercial trucking, eVTOL aviation, and advanced applications like mid-air 3D printing drones.
